Meet Gabby B

Brazilian Pop Powerhouse

Born in Goiania, Brazil, Gabby B is a multilingual pop powerhouse fluent in three languages and singing in four — English, French, Portuguese, and Spanish. Now based in Miami, she's built an empire of 5 million+ followers through viral music videos and electrifying performances.

Her singles have charted in 8 countries including the US, UK, Japan, Italy, and South Africa. From headlining the Stonewall Pride Festival to performing the National Anthem for the Miami Heat and Chicago Bulls, Gabby B is redefining what it means to be a global pop artist.
