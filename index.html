About Gabby B 💋

Gabby B is a Brazilian pop recording artist based in Miami, FL, with over 5 million followers. She is fluent in three languages and sings in four: English, French, Portuguese, and Spanish.

Her singles have charted in eight countries. She has shared the stage with Iza, Flo Rida, Ja Rule, and Justina Valentine. She headlined the Stonewall Pride Festival and Y100.7's Jingle Ball Tour in Miami.

Her single "Leche" was featured on NBC Miami and WSVN FOX's Deco Drive. "Si No Sabías" peaked at #13 on Latin Urban Radio Charts. In 2024, she attended Premios Juventud in Puerto Rico.
